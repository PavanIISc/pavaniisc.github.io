Pavan Kulkarni

Located in Bengaluru, INDIA

As a computer scientist with a strong foundation in applied mathematics, I am dedicated to using my skills to address significant challenges in the real world. At Bosch, I have the opportunity to channel my passion for AI and data analysis into meaningful research that directly impacts society.

My current research interests center around two crucial areas of AI - security and explainability. I am working to enhance the security of AI systems by detecting and preventing model extraction, evasion, and poisoning attacks. This work is crucial for ensuring that AI systems are trustworthy and safe for widespread use. Additionally, I am also exploring ways to increase the explainability of AI models, which is critical for ensuring that these systems are transparent and accountable.

In addition to my work in AI security and explainability, I also have experience in analyzing diverse data types using cutting-edge machine learning algorithms. My background includes text-to-speech synthesis, speech and audio processing, bioacoustics, and healthcare applications. By leveraging my expertise in these areas, I am able to provide valuable insights and solutions to complex problems.

Areas of Interest: AI/ML, Computer Vision, Speech/Audio Processing, Trustworthy AI, AI Security, Large Langauge Models (LLMs)
